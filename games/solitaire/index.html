How to play

  • Build tableau columns downward in alternating red and black. Move one card or a complete face-up run.
  • Only kings may enter empty tableau columns. Foundations build upward by suit, ace through king.
  • Click the stock to draw. When it empties, click again to recycle the waste with no pass limit.

Controls & scoring

Select a source and then a destination, or drag with a mouse. Double-click an exposed card to auto-place it on a foundation. Esc clears a selection, U undoes, and N deals a new game. Foundation +10, waste to tableau +5, exposing a card +5, recycle −100, foundation back −15.
