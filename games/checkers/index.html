Rules, AI & controls

Move & capture

  • Pieces move diagonally forward on dark squares. Captures are mandatory whenever available.
  • A jump can continue; take the full capture sequence with the same piece before the turn ends.

Kings & winning

  • Reach the far edge to crown a king. Kings move and capture in both directions.
  • You win by taking every opposing piece or leaving the opponent without a legal move.

Controls & AI

Click/tap a piece, then a gold destination. U undoes your last turn and N begins a new game. Easy is random; Medium values captures, crowns, and safety; Hard uses bounded minimax search.
