Rules, AI & controls

Place & move

  • Players take turns placing nine stones. Then stones move along a connected line to an empty point.
  • With only three stones left, you may fly to any empty point.

Mills & winning

  • Make three of your colour in a marked row to form a mill, then remove one opposing stone.
  • A stone outside a mill must be removed if possible. Win by reducing the opponent below three stones or blocking every move.

Controls & AI

Click/tap a source then a gold destination; after a mill, click a gold enemy stone to remove it. U undoes your prior turn; N starts over. Easy is random; Medium is tactical; Hard uses bounded minimax.
