Rules

  • Reveal every safe tile. A flag is decorative unless it is also correct.
  • Numbers show how many mines touch that tile in the eight surrounding cells.
  • If a revealed number already has that many adjacent flags, clicking it chords and opens the remaining neighbours.
  • Pause freezes the timer and the board. Handy for tea, less handy for courage.
