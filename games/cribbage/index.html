Rules, scoring & controls

Deal & peg

  • Each player gets six cards and discards two to the dealer’s crib. The non-dealer leads the pegging.
  • Alternate cards without passing 31. Score 2 for 15 or 31, 2/6/12 for pairs/triples/quads, and the length of a run. “Go” and the last card score 1.

Count the hands

  • Every combination totaling 15 scores 2; pairs score 2; runs score their length with multiplicity.
  • A four-card hand flush scores 4 (5 with a matching starter); a crib flush needs all 5. A jack matching the starter’s suit (“nobs”) scores 1. Cutting a jack scores the dealer 2.

Order & AI

The non-dealer counts first, then the dealer, then the crib. First to 121 wins immediately. Easy chooses random legal cards; Medium values expected points; Hard evaluates crib outcomes and likely pegging replies. Press N for a new game.
